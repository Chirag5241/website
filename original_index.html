Can collective human systems exhibit emergent "dreams"

I aim to formalize societal intelligence by exploring how collective human systems might exhibit emergent "dreams" that transcend individuals. I'm particularly intrigued by the question, If regions, with their diverse populations, had a mind, how would that mind dream?

Who am I?

I am a Computer Engineer and an Undergraduate Researcher at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign. I strive to constantly learn and innovate while impacting business and research outcomes through effective organization, prioritization, and execution.
